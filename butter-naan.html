Butter Naan

About Butter Naan

Butter Naan is a popular Indian flatbread that is soft and fluffy, made with refined flour (maida) and enriched with butter. It is typically served with various curries and is known for its slightly crispy exterior and soft, buttery interior.

Ingredients

  • Refined flour (maida)
  • Yogurt
  • Butter
  • Yeast
  • Salt
  • Water

Process

The dough for butter naan is made by mixing flour with yogurt, yeast, and water. It is allowed to rise before being rolled out and cooked in a tandoor or on a hot griddle. After cooking, it is brushed with melted butter for added flavor.
